Cream Cheese Frosting

Who doesn't love cream cheese frosting?

Cream cheese frosting will add that extra pizzaz to a cake without sending your guests to the hospital with insta-diabetes.

Easy to make, easy to eat!

Ingredients

  • 32oz Cream Cheese
  • 16 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, cream together cream cheese and butter
  2. mix in vanilla extract
  3. gradually mix in Powdered Sugar
  4. let frosting set in the fridge for a few hours to firm up
  5. use and store in fridge

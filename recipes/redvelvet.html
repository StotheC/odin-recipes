Red Velvet Cake

My wife's favorite!

A classic red velvet cake, with some extra tang in it.

Great with cream cheese frosting!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 Tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 Cups sugar
  • 1 cup Vegetable Oil or Canola Oil
  • 2 Eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1-2 oz red food coloring optional
  • .5 cups hot coffee
  • 1 teaspoon white distilled vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Grease abd flour 2 9-inch round cake pans
  3. whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, cocoa powder, and salt. Set aside
  4. combine sugar and vegetable oil in a large bowl
  5. mix in eggs, buttermilk, vanilla, and red food coloring into large bowl until combined
  6. stir coffee and white vinegar into large bowl
  7. combine wet ingredients with dry ingredients a little at a time, mixing after each addition until just combined
  8. pour evenly into each pan
  9. bake on middle rack for 30-40 minutes or until toothpick insterent in the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  10. let pans cool on a cooling rack until they are warm to the touch
  11. remove cakes from pan and put on cooling rack until completely cool
  12. frost!
